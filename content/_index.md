But there's so much out there, and we need your help! If you know of a great video that belongs here — whether it's a rare acoustic session, a legendary collaboration, or just a clip that made you smile — we'd love to hear about it.

You can also follow [@sheerio_online](https://x.com/sheerio_online) on X/Twitter to be notified whenever a new video is added.

## What is a Sheerio?

A **Sheerio** is a devoted fan of Ed Sheeran. The name was coined by Ed himself as a way to describe the community of people who love his music — a blend of "Sheeran" and the "-io" suffix that gives it a warm, personal feel.

Sheerios are known for their passion and loyalty, whether they're singing along at sold-out stadium shows, sharing clips of Ed's surprise appearances, or simply connecting over the songs that mean the most to them. If you're here, you're probably one of us.
