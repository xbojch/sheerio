A rare gem from early in Ed Sheeran's career — the full DVD recording of his live EP performance at The Bedford in London. An intimate and electrifying show that captures the raw talent that would soon take the world by storm! He played most of the songs in his now traditional fashion, just Ed with the loop pedal. On some songs he was backed by the band Remedies.
