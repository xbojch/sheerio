Ed and Martin Garrix finished Repeat It back in 2014 during the Multiply era after becoming friends on a night out in Amsterdam, and 12 years later it is finally out for Martin's 30th birthday. The video is stitched together from clips of both their lives over the last 12 years, and they played it live for the first time in Santiago the night before release.
