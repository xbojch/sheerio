Apple Music sat down with Ed Sheeran to celebrate "Shape of You" becoming the most-streamed song of the platform's entire first decade — a remarkable milestone for what's already one of the best-selling singles of all time. Ed reflects on the track's journey and what it means to him to have connected with so many listeners worldwide.
