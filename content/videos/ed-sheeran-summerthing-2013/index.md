Ed Sheeran played Alice's Summerthing on 24 June 2013, the free outdoor concert thrown by radio station Alice @ 97.3 in Sharon Meadow at Golden Gate Park in San Francisco. The crowd waited through the drizzling bay fog for a young Ed to arrive, and he repaid them with a full loop pedal set carried on just his acoustic guitar.

We have gathered many clips from that day here.
