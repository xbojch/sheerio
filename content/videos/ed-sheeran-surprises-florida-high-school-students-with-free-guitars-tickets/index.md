Ed Sheeran walks into a Florida high school band rehearsal and absolutely makes history for those students — donating guitars to the music department and handing out free tickets to his sold-out stadium show the very next day! He even stays to perform an acoustic "Perfect" for the stunned and tearful class in one of the most heartwarming surprise moments you'll ever see.
